Our Story



Founded in 2024, Beauty Bliss Cosmetics was born out of a desire to revolutionize the beauty industry. Our team of experts is dedicated to creating innovative products that cater to diverse skin types and tones.



We're passionate about empowering individuals to embrace their natural beauty. Our mission is to provide high-quality, cruelty-free cosmetics that inspire confidence and self-expression.










Natural cosmetics, you?!” - when I started working in the beauty industry as a product and brand manager, I often heard surprised reactions: “But you don’t look like that at all.”


That was true, I didn't look like that. In order to style myself in a way that made me feel comfortable, I sometimes used synthetic make-up myself. A contradiction that I was aware of. But what was the alternative? I wanted vegan, natural cosmetics and found: pale colors, crumbly creams, musty scents - hand on heart: many sustainable products were well-intentioned, but not exactly sexy. A bright, red lipstick without carmine? Impossible.


Since then, we have seen it as our mission to give the beauty industry new impulses. With the vision of freeing natural cosmetics from the dust of yesterday. And setting clear accents for a conscious and sustainable future in which we feel and look better than ever before.
